I’m Ying Zhou, a documentary film producer and journalist based in Boston. I recently graduated from Boston University with a master’s degree in mass communication.

Currently I’m working for the U.S. Public Interest Research Group (PIRG) Thinking BIG campaign, creating documentaries and explainer videos about human rights, e-waste, and environmental protection. In 2016, I worked as the first AD of the award-winning documentary series, The Great Meal.

As a documentary film producer and journalist, my interests are human rights, gender equality, public health, food safety, and AIDS prevention. In 2014 I interviewed Gao Yaojie.
